## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a real-world sales dataset to uncover:
- 📈 Monthly and yearly revenue trends
- 🏆 Top-performing products and categories
- 🗺️ Region-wise sales breakdown
- 💡 Key business KPIs (Total Revenue, Profit Margin, Growth Rate)

## 🔑 Key Insights Discovered

- Revenue grew 35% YoY from 2003 to 2004
- Classic Cars drive 35.8% of $8.3M total revenue
- USA, Spain, France = 59% of global revenue (concentration risk)
- $500K+ lost to cancellations — an operational improvement opportunity
